## Blend Recipe

### Ingredients:
- Sprinkle of "The Moon"
- A teaspoon of "The Sun"
- Spoonful of "The Star"
- A hint of "The Sky"

## How To Blend it?
I always loved the way stars twinkled upon the sky. It always remind me of my favorite painting "Starry Night" by Vincent Van Gogh. It's just rather...nice to just observe the star with a cup of coffee. Imagine the calm wind, silence, and warmth of drink. What a nice image.
